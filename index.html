Pillow.com

A Django-based web application designed to simplify the process for North-West University (NWU) students in finding accredited accommodation near their campuses.

Features

Student Accommodation Search

Our platform offers a convenient way for users to discover accredited student accommodations effortlessly. With our intuitive search feature, users can input any location or accommodation name of interest.

Our system then presents a comprehensive list of all accredited accommodations within the specified area or matching the accommodation name entered by the user. Simplifying the search for their ideal student housing

Accommodation Details Modal with Google Maps Integration

Upon clicking the "Details" button for any accommodation listed in the search results, users are presented with a convenient pop-up modal displaying comprehensive information about the selected accommodation. The modal includes essential details such as contact information, address, accommodation name, accreditation number, and more.

Additionally, our platform integrates seamlessly with Google Maps, providing users with a dynamic map view within the modal. Users can access directions directly from Google Maps, facilitating navigation to the chosen accommodation effortlessly.

About

Pillow.com aims to address the challenge faced by students at NWU Mafikeng Campus (South Africa) in finding accredited residences. Currently, students funded by the National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS) for accommodation support encounter difficulties in locating accredited residences, as it is a prerequisite for the accommodation to be accredited by NSFAS for funding eligibility. The existing solution involves a large PDF document containing comprehensive information about accredited residences, such as names, addresses, contact details, bed capacity, and accreditation numbers. However, the manual process of searching through this extensive PDF is time-consuming and cumbersome.


What Pillow.com Will Solve: The project aims to streamline and simplify the process of identifying accredited residences for NWU Mafikeng Campus students. By developing Pillow.com, students will have an accessible online platform where they can easily search, filter, and find accredited accommodations that meet their specific needs. The web application will provide a user-friendly interface, making it efficient for students to discover and select suitable residences that comply with NSFAS accreditation requirements.
